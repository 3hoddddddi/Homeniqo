Closet

Give your closet a light edit

A small closet reset for everyday clothes, shoes, towels, or shelves.

Edit by use, not by mood

Start with what you reach for often. Put useful items where they are easiest to see and move less-used pieces higher or farther back.

Make the front row useful

The first things you see should match your real week. This makes getting dressed or finding linens faster.

Do not turn it into a full project

A light edit should take a short amount of time. The goal is improvement, not perfection.
