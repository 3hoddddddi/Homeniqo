Daily reset

Start with one clear surface

A practical way to make a room feel calmer without reorganizing the whole house.

Choose the surface people notice first

A kitchen counter, entry table, coffee table, or desk can change how the room feels within a few minutes. Start with the area that catches your eye the most.

Remove, group, return

Take everything off, group similar items, then only return what the surface actually needs. This keeps the reset simple and visible.

Leave space on purpose

Empty space is useful. It gives the room room to breathe and makes daily cleaning faster.
