Quick guide

A faster way to reset one room.

Pick one room, choose one visible problem, and make the next reset easier. This guide is built for everyday rooms, not perfect show homes.

01

Choose the first visible problem

Start with the surface, pile, shelf, or corner that makes the room feel busy.

02

Remove what does not belong

Do not reorganize everything. Move unrelated items back to the room where they belong.

03

Give repeated items a home

If the same things keep appearing, they need a clear place that is easy to use.

04

Keep the reset small

A repeatable reset is more useful than a complicated system that only works once.
