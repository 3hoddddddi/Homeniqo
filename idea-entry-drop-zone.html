Entryway

Make a simple drop zone near the door

Keep keys, bags, shoes, mail, and daily items from spreading through the home.

Use the first place items land

A good drop zone should sit near the place people already put things down. The easier it is to use, the more likely it is to work.

Separate quick items from storage

Keys and sunglasses need fast access. Seasonal items and extra bags should live somewhere else.

Keep it visible

Hidden systems often become ignored systems. A hook, tray, or small shelf can be enough.
