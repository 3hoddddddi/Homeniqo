Kitchen

Keep the kitchen counter easier to use

Simple counter choices that make cooking, cleaning, and daily routines smoother.

Keep daily items close

The items used every day can stay visible. Everything else should earn its place on the counter.

Create a small working area

A clear section of counter makes cooking and cleanup feel easier. It also prevents every task from starting with moving clutter.

Reset the counter at night

A short evening reset gives the next morning a cleaner start.
